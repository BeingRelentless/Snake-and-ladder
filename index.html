How to Play Snake and Ladder

  • Objective: Be the first player to reach square 100!
  • Rolling Dice: Click "Roll Dice" to move your piece.
  • Ladders: If you land on the bottom of a ladder, you climb up to the top!
  • Snakes: If you land on a snake's head, you slide down to its tail!
  • Winning: The first player to reach or exceed square 100 wins!
  • Turn-based: Players take turns rolling the dice.
